ABOUT US

What is TED?

TED is committed to Ideas Worth Spreading. Each talk features fascinating ideas that demand to be heard. From scientific breakthroughs to inspiring human stories, TEDTalks has them all.

What is TEDxGeorgiaTech?

TEDxGeorgiaTech is an independently organized TEDx event in Atlanta, Georgia. We are proud to be run entirely by student volunteers. Our goal is to showcase ideas from our own community that inspire change on Georgia Tech campus and beyond.
