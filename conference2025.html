ABOUT

TEDxGeorgiaTech is an independently organized TEDx event in Atlanta, Georgia. We are proud to be run entirely by student volunteers. Our goal is to showcase ideas from our own community that inspire change on Georgia Tech campus and beyond. We host multiple events throughout the year, including a main TEDx conference in the Spring.

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, TED Talks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection. These local, self-organized events are branded TEDx, where x = independently organized TED event. Theme: Renaissance

The Renaissance was a period of profound change, creativity, and intellectual expansion. It was an era where art, science, and philosophy came together to reshape our world. This year, TEDxGeorgiaTech embraces the spirit of the Renaissance—where new ideas, innovation, and exploration define the future.

We believe that innovation is driven by collaboration and the pursuit of knowledge. Join us as we explore how the ideas born from this Renaissance spirit will spark change in today’s world.

Georgia Tech: A Modern Renaissance

Welcome to Georgia Tech, a place where the spirit of the Renaissance lives on through innovation, exploration, and collaboration. Georgia Tech is not just a campus; it's a community of thinkers, dreamers, and doers, much like the great minds of the Renaissance era.

As one of the nation's leading technical universities, Georgia Tech offers a unique environment for both intellectual and personal growth. Whether you're an aspiring engineer, scientist, artist, or entrepreneur, you'll find the tools and inspiration to shape the future.

This event will take place at the Cypress Theater in the John Lewis Student Center located at 351 Ferst Dr NW, Atlanta, GA 30332.
